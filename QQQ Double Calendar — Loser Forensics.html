Two flavors of catastrophe:

"Below Delta" losers (38 trades, avg -12.3%): VIX collapses -4.9 pts. Market rips up, call calendar gets crushed. Cluster in Aug + Nov.

The Golden Filter

4 simple rules that eliminate most losers while keeping the majority of winners.

1

Skip Aug / Nov / Dec entirely

These 3 months produce 50% losers and negative avg returns across all 3 years of data.

Eliminates ~50% loser-rate months
2

Don't open during FOMC week

FOMC week entries have 34.2% loser rate and +4.9% avg P/L — well below baseline.

34.2% → 28.5% loser rate
3

Never open Friday at 3 PM

45.2% of these trades lose. Weekend gap risk + baked-in theta decay = bad entry.

45.2% → 21.9% (use Fri 9:45 instead)
4

Require SLR ≥ 0.40 at entry

Below 0.40 means the position is already skewed — 45.3% become losers.

45.3% → 27.7% loser rate
